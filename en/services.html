Our Services

Our services are designed to meet the needs of the minority ethnic communities in the UK.

  • We provide non-judgmental support and awareness of mental health conditions.
  • We assist with vital information that is needed to guide the community to gain access for mental health services that is available to them through the NHS and other professional services.
  • We advocate, because we are passionate to see this community contribute, flourish and thrive in their host communities, for them to have fair and equal access to healthcare services.
  • We deliver innovative projects focused on providing mental health awareness that is targeted to change perceptions, break the silence and challenge the norms about mental health conditions of minority ethnic communities.
  • We deliver comprehensive understanding on the complex mental health needs of minority ethnic communities, which creates a better long-term policymaking decisions for the authorities which yields a positive impact on the health and wellbeing of the minority ethnic communities in the UK. A critical component of sustainable mental health policies and practices that is poverty reduction and a pivotal element to narrowing social and health inequities for all minority ethnic communities.
