Somali Mental Health Awareness Campaign

This campaign aims to encourage the Somali community to recognise and speak up about how their attitudes, words and behaviours can influence others’ experiences and their struggle with mental health problems.

Stay Settled in Newham EU Settlement Scheme

From July this year, EU nationals who haven’t completed their EU Settlement Scheme (EUSS) will face losing their rights to live and work in the UK.

Vaccine Community Engagement Programme

A joint vaccine community engagement project, supported by London Borough of Newham.

Digital Inclusion

A joint digital inclusion project in Custom House, delivering digital training, equipment and volunteering opportunities to the local residents. Supported by London Borough of Newham to become an official digital hub.

Shaah & Sheeko

A pilot project for the Somali community to reduce and prevent social isolation, particularly for people at risk, such as children and the elderly.
